About

I am a Graduate Software Engineer in IBM working as a Tester/Developer in the CICS TS Liberty team.

I graduated from Aberystwyth University with a 1st class Master of Engineering (MEng) in Software Engineering. I have performed research into dating artwork from catalogue photographs, the affects of Top Level Domains in the Domain Name System and the impacts and implications of Mobile Devices in monitoring people.

You can find more about my published work on the publications page and some of my lesser research on the essays page.
